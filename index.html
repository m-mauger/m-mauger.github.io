My Journey

I've always had a fascination for computers and technology. When I was younger I loved games and always wanted to work on my own. It wasn't until I had my first programming class in middle school that I found my love of programming and problem solving. Soon after highschool I attended Texas State University and got my BBA with a major in Computer Information Systems and a minor in Computer Science.

Right out of college I got accepted to work at HCLTech as a software developer for multiple years. It was a fun and valuable time for me as I got tons of real world experience working with clients such as USAA and Fidelity and their teams.

After working with them I feel very confident in my abilities as a developer and coming up with unique and creative solutions that other developers might not be able to see right away.
